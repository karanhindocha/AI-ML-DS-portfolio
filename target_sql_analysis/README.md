# Target – SQL Analysis

## Problem Statement
Target is a globally renowned retail brand known for delivering value, innovation, and an exceptional customer experience.  
This case study focuses on Target’s operations in **Brazil**, analyzing approximately **100,000 orders placed between 2016 and 2018**.

The dataset provides detailed insights across multiple business dimensions, including:
- Order lifecycle and status
- Pricing, payments, and freight performance
- Customer geography and demographics
- Product attributes
- Customer reviews and satisfaction

- The objective of this analysis is to leverage SQL to extract insights that help understand operational performance, customer behavior, and business growth trends in Brazil’s e-commerce market.

## Dataset
The data is available in 8 csv files:
customers.csv
sellers.csv
order_items.csv
geolocation.csv
payments.csv
reviews.csv
orders.csv
products.csv

## Key Insights
- **Year-on-year growth** indicates a steady increase in order volume.
- **Monthly seasonality** suggests recurring customer purchasing patterns.
- The majority of orders are placed **post-dawn**, indicating peak shopping hours.
- **São Paulo (SP)** dominates order volume, followed by **Rio de Janeiro (RJ)** and **Minas Gerais (MG)**.
- Strong growth trends highlight the **rapid expansion of Brazil’s e-commerce ecosystem**.

## Business Recommendations
- Introduce **optimized payment plans** to improve cash flow efficiency.
- Offer **customer incentives** for faster payment methods.
- Focus operational and marketing efforts on **high-performing states**.
